# Data-Visualization-Top-5-Teams-by-Medals-Paris-2024-Olympics

This project shows a visualization of the top 5 medal-winning countries from the Paris 2024 Olympics, showing both total medals and their distribution (gold, silver, bronze). The ranking is based on total medal count rather than official Olympic rules, which prioritize gold medals first, followed by silver and bronze.

Project Workflow
1. Extracted medal data from the official Olympics website.
2. Cleaned and structured the dataset in Excel.
3. Imported the dataset into Power BI for analysis.
4. Created a stacked column chart to visualize medal distribution.
5. Enhanced the final visual in Canva by adding country flags and the Paris 2024 Olympics logo.

Tools/Resources Used
- Olympics Official Website  
- Microsoft Excel
- Power BI
- Color-Hex
- Canva

Key Insight
- USA dominates overall with 126 total medals, far ahead of all other teams.
- USA and China are tied in gold medals (40 each), but the USA leads due to higher silver and bronze counts.
- There is a large gap between China and Great Britain, showing a top-heavy distribution.
- Great Britain (65) and France (64) have nearly identical total medals, indicating close competition.
- Australia has the lowest total among the top 5 but has a relatively balanced medal distribution.
